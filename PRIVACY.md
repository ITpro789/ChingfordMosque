# Privacy Policy

**Chingford Mosque Prayer Times**

_Last updated: 2026_

## Summary

The Chingford Mosque Prayer Times app does **not** collect, transmit, or share
any personal data. It has no user accounts, no analytics, and no third-party
tracking.

## What the app does with data

- **Prayer times.** The app fetches publicly published prayer times from
  [chingfordmosque.com](https://chingfordmosque.com) over an encrypted HTTPS
  connection. This is the same information anyone can view on the public
  website. No identifying information is sent with these requests beyond what a
  normal web request requires.
- **Local cache and settings.** Downloaded prayer-time schedules and your app
  settings (such as notification preferences) are stored **locally on your
  device**. They are never uploaded anywhere.
- **Notifications and alarms.** The app schedules local notifications and exact
  alarms on your device to remind you of prayer times (adhan). These run
  entirely on-device.
- **Qibla compass.** The Qibla direction feature reads your device's compass
  (magnetometer/accelerometer) sensors **locally** to point toward the Kaaba.
  No location data is collected, stored, or transmitted.

## What the app does NOT do

- No personal data is collected.
- No location data is collected or sent off the device.
- No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.
- No advertising.
- No sharing of any data with third parties.

## Data retention

Cache and settings remain on your device until you clear the app's data or
uninstall it.
